Blog

My current research insterest mainly lies in Reinforcement Learning which I believe is one of the key factors leading to general artificial intelligence. I have used RL soloving many 6G communication problems, for example, my undergraduate thesis established a novel 6G wireless communication system in which each IoT devices can allocate their spectrum and energy resources autonomously based on multi-agent deep reinforcement learning. I also published a patent reducing the sensing energy of SU by teaching them to learn the spectrum usage pattern of PU with DQN. RL has proved its ability of enabling agents to make decisions intelligently, which can even beat human kinds in certain fields, so I decide to explore more in improving the performance of RL algorithms, making them more interpretable, and applying them in real scenes in my graduate study.

Before that I mainly worked on Computer Vision, especially in classification, detection, segmentation, OCR, and 3D reconstruction. I conducted several projects about detecting niduses on CT images and identifying motions in figure skating in labs of EECS department. Besides, I have participated in the development of Face Recognition based Time and Attendance System as a algorithm engineer intern in Panasonic Corp., and Recognition of Japanese cursive script as a AI Lab researcher intern in Dalian Hi-think Computer Technology Corp..
